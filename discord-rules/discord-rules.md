# 🇺🇲 Discord Rules

{% hint style="danger" %}
The rules are subject to change at any time without notice; we encourage you to check them regularly.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">Part I :</mark> <mark style="color:orange;"></mark><mark style="color:orange;">**Your Profile**</mark>

### <mark style="color:orange;">**Article A : Profile Content**</mark>

* Your profile picture and/or banner must not contain obscene content, political references, or anything offensive toward an individual or group.
* Your username must not reference historical or political figures. It should begin with a character from an AZERTY keyboard to remain mentionable. Including another player’s personal or private information in your username is strictly prohibited.
* Your status and description must not include political content, hate speech, or scam links.
* Any form of disrespect toward an individual or group will result in sanctions.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Part II : Texte channels</mark>

### <mark style="color:orange;">Article A : Chat pollution</mark>

* Whether in-game or on Discord, spam and flood are prohibited.
* Posting links other than those related to the ChunkLock website, its voting pages, server IP, or official social networks is not allowed.
* Abusive mentions of roles or members are also strictly forbidden, including in tickets.

### <mark style="color:orange;">Article B : Expected conduct</mark>

* You must always communicate respectfully with other players.
* In tickets, insults, provocation, mockery, lies, or false accusations may lead to sanctions, up to and including a permanent ban, both in-game and on Discord.
* Politeness, respect, and professionalism are mandatory—especially when interacting with the staff team.

{% hint style="info" %}
A simple "Hello" when opening a ticket will make us immediately more attentive. **😉**
{% endhint %}

### <mark style="color:orange;">Article C : Topics</mark>

* Just like your profile, your messages must not contain any of the prohibited content mentioned above.
* Disrespecting or insulting another player is forbidden.
* Racism, homophobia, sexism, and any other form of hateful speech are strictly prohibited.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Part III : Voice channels</mark>

### <mark style="color:orange;">Article A : Conduct</mark>

* As in written chats, you must remain respectful toward other players.
* You are free to discuss any topic, but please consider the age of the participants in your voice channel.
* Private conversations can take place in the available limited-access channels.

### <mark style="color:orange;">Article B : Prohibitions</mark>

* Soundboards (sound effects, music, etc.) are not allowed.
* Yelling into your microphone to annoy or disturb others is forbidden.
* Sharing personal information over voice chat is also prohibited.
* It is forbidden to record a private conversation in order to make it public without the consent of all players present in voice chat.
